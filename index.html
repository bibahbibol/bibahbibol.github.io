INSPIRE

There are a some definitions of 'inspire' that i found on the internet. But on this blog, the word 'inspire' means to excite, encourage, or breathe life into. Inspire comes from the Latin word that means to inflame or to blow in to. When you inspire something, it is as if you are blowing air over a low flame to make it grow. PROBLEM

Problem means a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful and needing to be dealt with and overcome. Everyone must have a problem, whether it's with a friend, family, or strangers. Having a problem doesn't means the method of finishing it is avoiding or running away. People usually call it 'lame' which means that they cannot finish their problem by their own.

FINISHING METHOD

The word 'finish' means to bring (a task or activity) to an end; complete. The word 'method' means a particular form of procedure for accomplishing or approaching something, especially a systematic or established one. In a short way, finishing method means a process of completing a task or activity. Of course every situation or problems has a way to get out.
